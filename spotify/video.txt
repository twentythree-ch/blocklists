# Spotify Video Blocklist
# Blocks video content (Canvas, video podcasts) in the Spotify app
# Compatible with Pi-hole and other DNS-based blockers
#
# Usage in Pi-hole:
#   Settings > Blocklists > Add the raw URL of this file
#   e.g. https://raw.githubusercontent.com/twentythree-ch/blocklists/main/spotify/video.txt

# Spotify Canvas (short looping videos shown on track pages)
canvas.scdn.co
canvaz.scdn.co

# Spotify video content and video podcasts
video.spotify.com
seektables.scdn.co

# CDN endpoint used for Spotify video delivery
video-ak-spotify-com.akamaized.net
